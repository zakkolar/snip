Snip

Snip is an add-on designed to ease the process of turning paper worksheets into Google Forms. It provides a convenient way to crop scanned worksheets and save the resulting images to Google Drive in an organized, logical way.

If you have just installed Snip and do not see the "Snip images" button when you open the Snip menu, refresh the page.

Click here to open a Snip tutorial a new window.

Images are saved to a folder in your Google Drive and named based on their question number so that you can insert them into your forms. Click here to learn how to add images to questions and answers.

If you have installed the add-on but can only access the instructions:

You may need to enable Snip for the current form.

  1. Click on the add-ons menu (puzzle piece icon in the upper right side of the Forms editor)
  2. Click "Snip"
  3. Click "Help"
  4. Click "View in Store"
  5. Click the green "Manage" button
  6. Click "Use in this document" if there is not a checkmark next to it
  7. Refresh the Google Forms page
  8. Reopen the "Snip" add-on menu and click "Snip Images" to begin Snipping
